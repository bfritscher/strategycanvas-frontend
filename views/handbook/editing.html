Editing

A typical edit session start by adding factors and then drawing value curves onto the canvas. We will call each point of a value curve on a factor column an offering.

Factors

  • are added by clicking the big plus on the right
  • can be removed* or renamed by clicking on their label.
  • can be reordered by dragging them at their outside part of their column (blue on the picture)

Value curves

  • can be added manually by click the new button
  • are added automatically if a new offering is added to a factor, which already has offerings for all currently available value curves.
  • can me removed* or renamed by clicking on their label.
  • can have different colors, symbols and line styles by clicking on their symbol in the legend bar.

Offerings

  • can be added by clicking in the middle part of a factor column (red on the picture)
  • can be dragged to change their value.
  • can be removed by dragging them outside the canvas'bottom border.

*About removing

Removing does not delete the values, but only hides them from the canvas. Adding the same factor or value curve, will bring the connected data back onto the canvas.
